LUMEN Mind is installed

Voice control for Tesco, scrolling, and everyday sites — in Google Chrome on your Chromebook.

3 steps (like opening LUMEN on Windows)

  1. Pin LUMEN — click the puzzle icon in Chrome → pin LUMEN Mind.
  2. Open the panel — click the LUMEN icon → side panel opens on the right.
  3. Tap “Tap to speak” — say open Tesco, then search spaghetti, scroll, stop, up.

Tesco opens in a normal Chrome tab (not Edge). Scroll and search work on that tab.

Allow the microphone when Chrome asks.
